About Pitt

The University of Pittsburgh (Pitt), founded in 1787, is a leading public research university located in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Known for its strong academic programs and research excellence, Pitt offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional degrees, with standout programs in medicine, engineering, and public affairs. The campus is home to the iconic Cathedral of Learning, and its connection to the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center makes it a hub for healthcare innovation. With a vibrant student life and a strong community, Pitt is a key player in both education and research.
